## Overview

BRC Retail Extension is a comprehensive Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central app that provides advanced item variant management capabilities specifically designed for retail businesses. The solution extends Business Central's standard variant functionality with sophisticated multi-dimensional variant structures, automated barcode generation, matrix-style reporting, and seasonal delivery management.

The app solves the complex challenge of managing retail products with multiple characteristics (size, color, length, etc.) across various business processes including purchasing, sales, inventory management, and reporting.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Dimensional Variant Management**: Create and manage items with up to two variant dimensions (e.g., Size and Color) using customizable variant templates
- **Automated Variant Creation**: Automatically generate item variants from predefined variant templates with intelligent sorting and organization
- **Advanced Barcode Management**: Generate EAN codes with configurable check digit methods for items and variants with Code 128/39 barcode support
- **Matrix-Style Reporting**: Generate comprehensive matrix reports for sales orders, purchase orders, and invoices showing variants in grid format
- **Seasonal Management**: Track delivery seasons and automatically calculate seasonal information on sales and purchase documents
- **Brand Management**: Organize items by retail brands with dedicated brand management functionality
- **Inventory Matrix Views**: View inventory levels across variants and locations in intuitive matrix layouts
- **Multi-Language Support**: Full translation capabilities for variant names and descriptions across multiple languages
- **Warehouse Integration**: Specialized warehouse activity reports with barcode labels for efficient picking and inventory management

## Getting Started

Before implementing BRC Retail Extension, ensure your Business Central environment meets the minimum requirements and follow the setup guide for optimal configuration.

**Prerequisites:**
- Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central version 24.0 or higher
- Appropriate Business Central licenses for inventory and sales management
- Understanding of your product variant structure (dimensions like size, color, style)
